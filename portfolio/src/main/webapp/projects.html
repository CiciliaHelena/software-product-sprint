Outside school

AKUL

From May 2020

Akul is an abbreviation of “anak kuliah” which also means university student. This is an educational project on a youtube channel that discusses the tips and tricks on how to get into a famous university around the world. Not only that, it also covers the information about scholarship, school fees, and anything you need to know to survive financially. The targeted audiences are Indonesian youths who want to continue their studies to the top universities, yet do not have enough information to guide them through the process.

Indonesian Cultural Night (ICN) Production

Aug 2019- Feb 2020

ICN is an annual musical theatre production that brings a new perspective on Indonesian folklore and history to the Singapore community. I participated as a sound effect designer, and collaborated with the scriptwriter to design the first real-time sound effects in ICN history! That actually took me weeks to practice to press and start the sound based on the character's movement. I had 4 important fighting scenes to focus on that I worried the most, as I need to remember long choreographies for each. I was so happy and grateful for the show, all our hard work paid off.


Proyek Malin

Aug 2017 - Dec 2017

We led several teaching sessions for five days covering handcrafting, norms, virtues, and leadership through games and activities for 35 marginal children of age 5-18. Interactions with children are very challenging as we need to be a good example for them. We must keep in mind all the time that our wrongdoing towards them is very dangerous, as it can ruin their confidence and even their future.
